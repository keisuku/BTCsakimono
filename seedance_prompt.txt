【メインプロンプト（185語版・推奨）】

@Image1 storyboard reference. @Image2 character reference.

Photorealistic cinematic style, shallow depth of field, anamorphic lens flare.
A young woman with long white-silver hair, red fabric headband,
red sleeveless dress, flowing blue cape with blue interior,
blue gem pendant necklace, white long gloves past the elbow,
white thigh-high socks, vivid blue eyes. Dark cosmic void with
faint hexagonal blockchain grid.

0-2s: Hand on glowing blue pendant, eyes closed then snapping open
glowing bright blue. Blue luminous energy threads stream upward
from pendant. Silver hair floats upward catching blue light.
Slow camera push-in toward her face.

2-5s: Arms raised wide. Blue energy threads connect to floating
octahedral crystal nodes forming a spherical validator network.
She shouts with fierce determination. Nodes flash in rapid
sequence like consensus wave. Camera pulls to wide low-angle shot.

5-8s: Nodes converge above her hands. Glowing data particles stream
inward. Massive crystalline Ethereum octahedron forges at convergence.
Octahedron splits into orange and blue halves, merges back with
brilliant flash and expanding shockwave ring. Slow orbital camera.

8-10s: Shockwave transforms void into vast crystalline geometric
city of blue light stretching to horizon. She floats center, cape
flowing majestically, completed octahedron orbiting above like crown.
Final hero pose, hand extended toward camera, confident expression.
Static wide shot, dramatic backlighting.

Maintain face and clothing consistency, no facial distortion,
stable human anatomy, natural smooth movements. Cinematic dramatic
lighting, blue-indigo dominant palette, orange accent only at merge.
Epic orchestral crescendo, crystalline chimes, deep boom on shockwave.
