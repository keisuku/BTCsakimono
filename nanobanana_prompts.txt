■■■ 方法A: 1枚の画像に9コマグリッドを直接生成 ■■■

【プロンプト（Image 1: メインストーリーボード）】

Create a 3x3 grid storyboard image, photorealistic cinematic
style, consistent character across all 9 panels. The character
is a young woman cosplayer with long white-silver hair, red
headband, red sleeveless dress, blue cape, blue gem pendant,
white opera gloves, white thigh-high socks, blue eyes.

Panel layout (left to right, top to bottom):

1. Medium shot: She stands in dark cosmic void with faint blue
   hexagonal grid. Right hand on glowing blue gem pendant, eyes
   closed. Serene, anticipatory mood.

2. Close-up face/chest: Eyes snapping open, glowing vivid blue.
   Blue luminous energy threads streaming upward from pendant.
   White hair floating upward in blue light.

3. Wide shot: Both arms spread wide. Blue energy threads extend
   outward connecting to small glowing octahedral crystal nodes
   floating in spherical arrangement around her. Network of light
   lines between nodes. Ethereal blue magic circle aesthetic.

4. Medium shot, low angle heroic framing: Mouth open wide shouting.
   Fierce determined expression. Red dress contrasts against blue
   energy. The crystal nodes flash with light in sequence around her.

5. Wide shot: All crystal nodes converging upward above her raised
   hands. Streams of glowing data particles flowing along connection
   lines toward center. Small brilliant Ethereum-shaped octahedron
   crystallizing above her hands. Cape flaring dramatically upward.

6. Close-up: Massive faceted Ethereum octahedron crystal above her
   white-gloved hands. Translucent crystal with swirling light data
   inside. Hands framing from below, fingers spread. Shallow depth
   of field, dreamy bokeh.

7. Wide shot: The octahedron split into two halves — one glowing
   orange, one glowing blue — slamming back together with blinding
   white flash. Circular shockwave ring expanding outward. Most
   dramatic and intense panel.

8. Wide shot: Dark void transformed into vast crystalline geometric
   city of blue light stretching to horizon. She floats at center,
   cape flowing majestically. Completed octahedron orbiting above
   her head like a crown.

9. Final hero shot: One hand extended toward viewer palm-out, confident
   calm expression. Massive Ethereum octahedron floating behind her
   like a sun. Geometric crystal city to horizon. Dramatic backlight.
   Faint arc of light reading "TORICO" across the sky like constellation.

Consistent lighting (blue-indigo dominant, cinematic). High detail,
4K quality, professional VFX film aesthetic. Each panel clearly
separated by thin borders.

■■■ 方法B: 個別パネルを生成して後で結合 ■■■
（より高品質だが手間がかかる）

各パネルを個別に生成し、Canva等で3x3グリッドに結合する方法。
以下は各パネルの個別プロンプト：

---

【Panel 1 — 構え】
Photorealistic cinematic shot. A young woman cosplayer with long
white-silver hair, red headband, red sleeveless dress, blue cape,
blue gem pendant, white gloves, white thigh-high socks stands in
a dark cosmic void. Faint blue hexagonal grid pattern in background.
Her right hand rests on the glowing blue gem pendant at her chest.
Eyes closed, serene expression. Medium shot, soft blue rim lighting.
4K, shallow depth of field, anamorphic lens.

---

【Panel 2 — 覚醒】
Photorealistic cinematic close-up. Same young woman, white-silver
hair now floating upward catching blue light. Her eyes have just
snapped open — vivid glowing blue irises. Thin blue luminous energy
threads stream upward from the blue gem pendant at her chest.
Expression of awakening power. Close-up on face and upper body.
Blue volumetric lighting from below. 4K, anamorphic bokeh.

---

【Panel 3 — ネットワーク展開】
Photorealistic cinematic wide shot. Same young woman, both arms
spread wide. Blue energy threads extend outward from her pendant
in all directions. The threads connect to 8-12 small floating
octahedral crystal nodes arranged in a sphere around her. Nodes
glow blue-white and are connected to each other by thin light lines,
forming a network/magic circle. Dark cosmic void background with
hexagonal grid now glowing brighter. 4K, dramatic wide framing.

---

【Panel 4 — 叫び】
Photorealistic cinematic medium shot, low angle heroic framing.
Same young woman, mouth open wide, shouting with fierce determined
expression. Red dress creates striking contrast against surrounding
blue energy. The octahedral crystal nodes around her flash with
light in rapid sequence. Light ripple propagating through the
network. Dramatic rim lighting. 4K, anamorphic lens flare.

---

【Panel 5 — 収束】
Photorealistic cinematic wide shot. Same young woman, arms raised
upward. All octahedral crystal nodes converging toward a single
point above her hands. Streams of glowing particles (like data/code)
flowing along the connection lines toward the convergence point.
A small, intensely bright Ethereum-shaped octahedron begins
crystallizing at the focal point. Her blue cape flares dramatically
upward from energy updraft. 4K, volumetric blue light.

---

【Panel 6 — 鍛造】
Photorealistic cinematic close-up. A massive faceted crystalline
Ethereum octahedron hovers above white-gloved hands. The crystal
is translucent with swirling galaxies of light data inside — like
smart contracts being written in light. The surface shows geometric
diamond facets. Her white-gloved hands frame it from below, fingers
spread. Shallow depth of field with beautiful bokeh. 4K.

---

【Panel 7 — The Merge ★クライマックス】
Photorealistic cinematic VFX wide shot. A massive crystalline
octahedron has split into two halves: left half glows intense orange,
right half glows intense blue. They are slamming back together in the
center with a blinding white flash at the contact point. A circular
shockwave ring of light expands outward from the fusion. Same young
woman silhouetted below. Most dramatic and intense lighting. 4K,
high-contrast, lens flare.

---

【Panel 8 — 世界変容】
Photorealistic cinematic wide aerial shot. Where there was dark void,
now a vast crystalline geometric city of blue light rises to the
horizon. Tall prismatic structures, geometric spires, light bridges.
Same young woman floats at the center, cape flowing majestically.
The completed Ethereum octahedron orbits above her head like a
luminous crown. Blue and white color palette. 4K, epic scale.

---

【Panel 9 — アイコン（最終フレーム/サムネイル）】
Photorealistic cinematic hero shot. Same young woman in powerful
stance. One hand extended toward the viewer, palm out, presenting
her creation. Confident, calm, visionary expression. The massive
Ethereum octahedron floats behind her radiating light like a sun.
The geometric crystal city of blue light extends to the horizon.
Dramatic backlight silhouette edge. A faint arc of light across
the sky suggests the text "TORICO" like a constellation. 4K,
iconic composition, poster-worthy framing.
